Intro

In pursuit of discovering "Nothing" - There is no such as nothing. Even in vacuum, virtual particles are constantly created and annihilated. Their existence is not a mathematical myth. Rather, the effects they create are confirmed by several experiments to a high degree of accuracy."

BTW, check my awesome work.

A postdoctoral researcher with six years of experience in various experiemnts in Particle Physics and Nuclear Physics. Currently working at Wayne State University, Detroit on the STAR (Solenoidal Tracker at RHIC) experiment - a heavy ion collision experiment at ultra-relativistic energies running in Brookhaven National lab (BNL) New York. Expertise in data analysis and software development for high precision Intensity-Frontier experiments in particle physics and high energy nucler physics experiments. Experienced in detector callibrations, simulations, numerical analysis, data collection, designing and programming a DAQ (data acquasition systems) and web-based DQM (data quality management) for monitoring online data collection.
